## Phase 1: Think Like a Scientist

### Step 1.1 — Identify the Research Problem

Based on the provided source material detailing the Secret Space Program (SSP), extraterrestrial contact, and advanced technologies, here are 5 potential research problems formulated for qualitative inquiry:

* **Problem 1: The Socio-Political Impact of Covert Extraterrestrial Agreements**
* There is a lack of understanding regarding the geopolitical consequences of secret treaties between national military branches (such as the US Air Force and Navy) and differing extraterrestrial factions, such as the Nordics or Draconians.
* Research is needed to explore how these covert alliances influence global power dynamics, military compartmentalization, and hidden conflicts on Earth and in space.


* **Problem 2: The Suppression and Application of Advanced Reverse-Engineered Technologies**
* Secret space programs, managed by military forces and corporate conglomerates, have reportedly acquired and reverse-engineered exotic technologies, including antigravity propulsion, temporal drives, and advanced healing devices.
* A significant research problem lies in analyzing the ethical implications and the societal impact of suppressing these advanced, paradigm-shifting technologies from public and civilian use.


* **Problem 3: The Historical and Survival Function of Ancient Space Arks**
* Evidence suggests the existence of massive, ancient space arks hidden on Earth, the Moon, and other planetary bodies, which are designed to preserve human, animal, and plant life during catastrophic events.
* Investigating the origins, operational mechanics, and historical deployments of these arks could illuminate previously unknown cycles of planetary destruction and the reseeding of human civilizations.


* **Problem 4: The Psychological and Physiological Impact of Covert Military Abductions (MILABs)**
* Individuals, such as the contactee "JP," report being subjected to covert military abductions where they undergo memory wiping, forced off-world missions, and injections with classified substances.
* Researching the methods, motives, and long-term psychological effects of these unauthorized programs on civilian subjects represents a critical gap in understanding covert intelligence operations.


* **Problem 5: The Weaponization of Weather Modification and Directed Energy Technologies**
* Rogue Special Access Programs (SAPs) and the "Deep State" are alleged to possess and utilize advanced weather manipulation technologies to orchestrate false flag operations and target rival military installations.
* Analyzing the capabilities and deployment of such technologies—such as the alleged steering of Hurricane Irma towards MacDill Air Force Base—is essential for understanding modern covert warfare and engineered environmental anomalies.
