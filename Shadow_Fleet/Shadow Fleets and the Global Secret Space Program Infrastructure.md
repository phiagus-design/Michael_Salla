# Shadow Fleets and the Global Secret Space Program Infrastructure

Based on the provided texts by Michael Salla, the "Secret Space Program" (SSP) is not a single, unified entity, but rather a complex, highly classified network of multiple competing and cooperating space programs developed by national governments, transnational corporations, and human breakaway civilizations,. These programs utilize exotic technologies—such as antigravity propulsion, temporal (time-travel) drives, and age-regression—acquired through reverse-engineering retrieved spacecraft and through alliances with various extraterrestrial species.

The corpus identifies five modern secret space programs, as well as several ancient programs, operating within and beyond our solar system:

1. The Vril Society, Nazi SS, and the "Dark Fleet"
The genesis of the modern secret space programs traces back to the Weimar Republic in 1920s Germany, where secret societies (the Vril, Thule, and Black Sun societies) began developing flying saucer prototypes,,. Led by psychic medium Maria Orsic, the Vril Society channeled technical instructions from human-looking "Nordic" extraterrestrials from the Aldebaran star system,. In the 1930s and 1940s, the Nazi SS, under Heinrich Himmler and Hans Kammler, sought to weaponize these antigravity technologies for the war effort,,.
Recognizing their impending defeat in Europe, the Germans relocated their most advanced technologies and personnel to hidden underground bases in Antarctica and South America. There, they allied with an aggressive, imperialistic extraterrestrial race known as the Draco Reptilians. This Antarctic-based German program defeated a 1946-1947 U.S. Navy expedition (Operation Highjump) and eventually evolved into the "Dark Fleet",. The Dark Fleet operates primarily outside our solar system, effectively serving as a mercenary space force to assist the Draconians in galactic conquest and a vast galactic slave trade,,,.

2. Solar Warden (The U.S. Navy Space Fleet)
The United States began its own SSP by playing catch-up to the Germans. During WWII, U.S. Navy spies embedded in Nazi Germany reported back on the advanced antigravity craft being developed,. Through Operation Paperclip and reverse-engineering efforts at classified facilities like Area 51 (S-4), the U.S. sought to develop its own antigravity craft,.
The U.S. Navy spearheaded the first major American SSP, known as "Solar Warden". Aided covertly by Nordic extraterrestrials who infiltrated aerospace companies like Douglas Aircraft Company, the Navy designed kilometers-long space battle groups (carriers and cruisers),. Solar Warden became operational in the early 1980s, functioning as an aging but heavily upgraded fleet focused on policing the solar system, protecting against extraterrestrial intruders, and defending Earth,.

3. The Interplanetary Corporate Conglomerate (ICC)
As the U.S. military-industrial complex reverse-engineered advanced technologies, a massive corporate-run space program emerged, known as the Interplanetary Corporate Conglomerate (ICC),. Formed by transnational defense contractors (such as Lockheed Martin and Northrop Grumman) and managed by a shadowy control group known as Majestic-12 (MJ-12) or the Cabal/Illuminati, the ICC amassed power that ultimately eclipsed the military-run Solar Warden program,,.
The ICC possesses a huge infrastructure in space, owns most of the bases on Mars, and manufactures extremely advanced technology to barter with over 900 extraterrestrial civilizations,,. Egregiously, the ICC is deeply involved in a galactic slave trade, bartering abducted humans and forced labor for advanced extraterrestrial technologies, and running its Mars colonies as ruthless corporate dictatorships,.

4. The U.S. Air Force / NRO / CIA Space Program
Distinct from the Navy's deep-space Solar Warden fleets, the U.S. Air Force, National Reconnaissance Office (NRO), Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA), and CIA run a parallel, lower-level secret space program focused on near-Earth orbit, space surveillance, and global covert operations,.
This program operates advanced stealth space stations and utilizes reverse-engineered antigravity craft, such as the TR-3B "flying triangles" and flying rectangular weapons platforms,,. Because of intense compartmentalization and "need to know" security protocols, personnel within this USAF-led program are often completely unaware of the Navy's more advanced Solar Warden program or the existence of the ICC,.

5. The Global Galactic League of Nations
Following a 1987 United Nations speech by President Ronald Reagan warning of a hypothetical alien threat, a massive international space program was formed. This program, referred to as the Global Galactic League of Nations, acts as a "NATO-type" SSP that operates almost entirely outside the solar system. It was created to secure the cooperation and silence of all major Earth nations by giving them a stake in advanced interstellar technologies and joint space exploration,.

6. Ancient Earth Breakaway Civilizations
Beyond the modern programs, there are 5 to 7 secret space programs belonging to ancient Earth civilizations (such as the Mayans) that survived past planetary cataclysms using advanced space arks. Some of these groups continue to operate today. Historically, many of these ancient breakaway civilizations have been deceptive, presenting themselves as extraterrestrial "gods" or benevolent aliens from other star systems to manipulate modern humanity and the modern SSPs.

7. Rogue Special Access Programs (SAPs)
There are also smaller, highly secretive, and heavily compartmentalized Special Access Programs run by the Secret Earth Governments, Syndicates, and World Military Forces. These rogue SAPs have their own independent, unacknowledged "black budget" funding mechanisms and operate outside traditional military or government oversight. They possess extremely advanced technologies and are involved in orchestrating false flag operations (such as weather manipulation or space-based attacks) and pushing the dangerous integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI), which other SSPs view as an existential threat.

## Current Status and "Full Disclosure"
These distinct Secret Space Programs frequently find themselves in conflict. A "Secret Space Program Alliance" (comprising Solar Warden, Earth-based "White Hats", and defectors from the ICC and the Dark Fleet) has formed to oppose the oppressive control of the Cabal/Illuminati and the Draconian Federation,. Protected by a highly advanced group of 6th- to 9th-density beings called the "Sphere Being Alliance," the SSP Alliance is currently pushing for a "Full Disclosure Event". This event aims to reveal the existence of extraterrestrials, dump massive amounts of classified documents on the internet, end the galactic slave trade, and release suppressed technologies (like free energy and age-regression) to transition humanity into a liberated "Star Trek" civilization,.
